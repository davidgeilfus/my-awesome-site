Features

Prospecting

Website Domain Search

Simply visit the prospect’s website and click on the extension icon on the top right corner of your browser.

We’ll provide all email addresses associated with that domain name. Just pick the ones you’re interested in!

Find Prospects on Professional Networks

Our Google Chrome extension is directly integrated in the most famous business oriented social networks. Fire up the extension and find names, emails, job titles and companies in 1 click! Test

Find Email Addresses in Bulk

Our extension allows you to pick prospects on a page individually or all at once!

Emailing

Schedule Your Emails

Don’t want to start sending emails right away? No problem, schedule your campaigns and Prospect.io will launch them later.

This allows you to send cold emails at the right time, by avoiding weekends for example.

Emails Sequences

Create sequences of emails to be sent and choose to automatically stop the campaign when the prospect replies, clicks, converts, etc.
